TechNova Solutions - Employee Handbook
Document: Data Privacy Policy
Last Updated: December 2024
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Data Privacy Policy

1.1 Overview
TechNova Solutions is committed to protecting the privacy of our team members, customers, and partners. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

1.2 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All TechNova team members worldwide
- All personal data processed by TechNova
- All systems and applications that handle personal data
- Third-party vendors who process data on our behalf

2. Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Team Member Data
We collect and process the following team member data:
- Contact information (name, address, phone, email)
- Employment information (position, department, manager)
- Compensation and benefits data
- Performance and evaluation records
- Time and attendance data
- Background check results
- Emergency contact information
- Government-issued IDs and tax information

2.2 Customer Data
We process customer data including:
- Account and contact information
- Usage and activity data from our products
- Support and communication records
- Payment and billing information
- Feedback and survey responses

2.3 Website and Marketing Data
We collect from visitors:
- IP address and device information
- Website usage and analytics data
- Cookie and tracking data
- Marketing preferences and consent
- Event registration information

3. How We Use Personal Data

3.1 Lawful Bases for Processing
We process personal data based on:
- Contract: Necessary to fulfill employment or service agreements
- Legal Obligation: Required by applicable laws and regulations
- Legitimate Interest: To operate and improve our business
- Consent: Where explicitly provided by the individual

3.2 Purposes of Processing
Team member data is used to:
- Administer employment and payroll
- Manage benefits and leave
- Conduct performance reviews
- Ensure workplace safety and security
- Comply with legal and tax requirements
- Communicate company information

Customer data is used to:
- Provide and improve our products and services
- Process transactions and billing
- Provide customer support
- Send relevant communications (with consent)
- Analyze usage for product improvement
- Comply with legal requirements

4. Data Protection Principles

4.1 Core Principles
TechNova adheres to these data protection principles:

Lawfulness and Transparency
- Process data only with valid legal basis
- Be clear about how data is used
- Maintain accessible privacy notices

Purpose Limitation
- Collect data only for specified purposes
- Don't use data for incompatible purposes
- Document all data processing activities

Data Minimization
- Collect only necessary data
- Don't retain data longer than needed
- Regularly review and purge unnecessary data

Accuracy
- Keep personal data accurate and current
- Enable individuals to update their information
- Correct inaccuracies promptly

Security
- Implement appropriate technical safeguards
- Maintain organizational security measures
- Regularly assess and improve protections

Accountability
- Document compliance efforts
- Maintain records of processing activities
- Be prepared to demonstrate compliance

5. Data Subject Rights

5.1 Individual Rights
Individuals have the right to:

Right of Access
- Request a copy of personal data we hold
- Understand how their data is being processed
- Receive this information within 30 days

Right to Rectification
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Complete incomplete personal data

Right to Erasure
- Request deletion of personal data
- Also known as "right to be forgotten"
- Subject to legal retention requirements

Right to Restrict Processing
- Limit how we use their data
- While disputes are being resolved

Right to Data Portability
- Receive data in a structured format
- Transfer data to another organization

Right to Object
- Object to certain types of processing
- Including direct marketing

5.2 Exercising Rights
To exercise privacy rights:
- Team members: Submit request through Workday or email privacy@technova.com
- Customers: Use the privacy portal at privacy.technova.com or email privacy@technova.com
- Response provided within 30 days

6. Data Sharing

6.1 Internal Sharing
Personal data may be shared internally:
- Within HR for employment administration
- With managers for performance management
- With IT for system access and security
- With Finance for payroll and benefits
- On a need-to-know basis only

6.2 External Sharing
We may share data with:
- Service providers who assist our operations
- Legal and regulatory authorities when required
- Professional advisors (legal, accounting, insurance)
- With consent of the individual

We do NOT:
- Sell personal data to third parties
- Share data for unrelated marketing purposes
- Transfer data without appropriate safeguards

6.3 International Transfers
When transferring data internationally:
- Standard Contractual Clauses in place with recipients
- Data Processing Agreements with all vendors
- Compliance with applicable transfer mechanisms
- Regular assessment of recipient country protections

7. Data Security

7.1 Technical Measures
We implement:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Intrusion detection and prevention
- Secure backup and recovery

7.2 Organizational Measures
We maintain:
- Privacy and security training for all staff
- Access limited to authorized personnel
- Confidentiality agreements
- Incident response procedures
- Regular policy reviews

8. Data Retention

8.1 Retention Periods
We retain personal data only as long as necessary:
- Employment records: 7 years after termination
- Payroll records: 7 years per tax requirements
- Performance records: 5 years after termination
- Customer account data: Duration of relationship plus 3 years
- Marketing data: Until consent withdrawn

8.2 Data Disposal
When data is no longer needed:
- Electronic data securely deleted
- Physical documents shredded
- Backup systems purged according to schedule
- Third parties required to delete upon termination

9. Data Breach Response

9.1 What Is a Breach
A data breach is:
- Unauthorized access to personal data
- Accidental loss or destruction of data
- Disclosure of data to unauthorized recipients
- Alteration of data without authorization

9.2 Reporting a Breach
If you suspect a data breach:
1. Report immediately to privacy@technova.com
2. Include all known details
3. Do not attempt to investigate yourself
4. Preserve any evidence
5. Maintain confidentiality

9.3 Breach Response Process
Upon notification:
- Security team investigates within 24 hours
- Assessment of scope and impact
- Containment measures implemented
- Notification to affected individuals if required
- Regulatory notification within required timeframes
- Post-incident review and remediation

10. Cookies and Tracking

10.1 Website Cookies
Our websites use cookies for:
- Essential site functionality
- Analytics and performance measurement
- Marketing and advertising (with consent)

10.2 Cookie Consent
Users can manage cookie preferences:
- Cookie consent banner on first visit
- Cookie settings available in website footer
- Browser settings to block cookies
- Opt-out links for advertising cookies

11. Training and Awareness

11.1 Required Training
All team members must complete:
- Data Privacy Fundamentals (upon hire)
- Annual privacy refresher training
- Role-specific training for data handlers

11.2 Privacy Champions
Each department has a Privacy Champion who:
- Serves as privacy point of contact
- Assists with privacy questions
- Escalates issues to Privacy team
- Promotes privacy awareness

12. Compliance

12.1 Regulatory Framework
TechNova complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other applicable state and national laws

12.2 Documentation
We maintain:
- Records of processing activities
- Data protection impact assessments
- Vendor agreements and assessments
- Training and compliance records
