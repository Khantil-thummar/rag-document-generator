Document: Code of Conduct
Last Updated: December 2024
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Code of Conduct

1.1 Our Commitment
At TechNova Solutions, we are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and inclusive work environment. Every team member, contractor, and partner is expected to uphold the highest standards of professional conduct, treating others with dignity and respect regardless of age, gender, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic.

1.2 Expected Professional Behavior
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive work environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language in all communications
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback and criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the team and the company
- Showing empathy and understanding towards colleagues
- Maintaining confidentiality of sensitive company and client information
- Being punctual and reliable in meetings and deliverables

1.3 Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are strictly prohibited at TechNova Solutions:
- Harassment of any kind, including verbal, physical, or visual harassment
- Discriminatory comments, jokes, or actions based on protected characteristics
- Bullying, intimidation, or threatening behavior
- Sharing others' private information without explicit permission
- Use of inappropriate language or imagery in the workplace
- Retaliation against anyone who reports concerns in good faith
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

1.4 Workplace Conduct Standards
All TechNova team members are expected to:
- Arrive prepared and engaged for scheduled meetings
- Communicate professionally in all channels (email, Slack, video calls)
- Respect work-life boundaries and avoid contacting colleagues outside business hours unless urgent
- Keep shared spaces (physical and virtual) clean and organized
- Follow all company policies and procedures
- Report any violations of this code promptly

1.5 Scope of This Policy
This Code of Conduct applies to all work-related settings, including:
- TechNova office locations and remote workspaces
- Company-sponsored events and meetings
- Online platforms and communication channels
- Business travel and client sites
- Social media when representing TechNova
- Any situation where you are acting on behalf of the company

1.6 Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it through one of the following channels:
- Direct conversation with your manager (if appropriate)
- Human Resources department at hr@technova.com
- Anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-TECHNOVA
- Online reporting portal at ethics.technova.com

All reports will be investigated promptly and confidentially. TechNova prohibits retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report.

1.7 Consequences of Violations
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, including:
- Verbal or written warning
- Mandatory training or counseling
- Suspension with or without pay
- Termination of employment
- Legal action where appropriate

The severity of consequences will depend on the nature and frequency of the violation.

1.8 Manager Responsibilities
Managers at TechNova have additional responsibilities to:
- Model exemplary conduct in all interactions
- Address concerns and complaints promptly and fairly
- Create an environment where team members feel safe to speak up
- Ensure team members understand and follow this Code of Conduct
- Escalate serious matters to Human Resources immediately

1.9 Annual Acknowledgment
All team members are required to review and acknowledge this Code of Conduct annually. New hires must complete this acknowledgment during their first week of employment.

1.10 Policy Updates
This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed. Team members will be notified of any significant changes. Questions about this policy can be directed to hr@technova.com.
