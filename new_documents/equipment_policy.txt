1. Equipment Policy Overview

1.1 Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines for the use, care, and return of company-provided equipment and technology. TechNova provides equipment to enable team members to perform their jobs effectively while maintaining security and accountability.

1.2 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All company-owned hardware (laptops, monitors, phones)
- Software and subscriptions
- Office equipment and furniture (if applicable)
- Any technology assets provided by TechNova

2. Equipment Provided

2.1 Standard Equipment
New team members receive:

Laptop:
- Choice of Mac (MacBook Pro) or Windows (Dell XPS/Lenovo ThinkPad)
- Refreshed every 3-4 years or as needed
- Includes charger and necessary adapters

Accessories (as needed):
- External monitor for remote workers
- Keyboard and mouse
- Webcam and headset
- Laptop stand

2.2 Role-Specific Equipment
Some roles receive additional equipment:
- Engineers: Second monitor, ergonomic accessories
- Sales: Mobile phone or phone stipend
- Design: Tablet, drawing equipment
- Executive: Premium communication equipment

2.3 Requesting Equipment
To request equipment:
1. Submit request through IT portal
2. Provide business justification
3. Manager approval required for non-standard items
4. IT will review and fulfill approved requests

3. Home Office Setup

3.1 Home Office Stipend
Remote team members receive:
- One-time stipend: $1,000 for home office setup
- Eligible expenses: Desk, chair, lighting, accessories
- Submit receipts through expense system
- Must be used within first 6 months

3.2 Recommended Setup
We recommend:
- Ergonomic chair with lumbar support
- Desk at proper height
- External monitor at eye level
- Good lighting for video calls
- Quiet space for focused work

3.3 Ergonomic Assessment
Request an ergonomic assessment:
- Available to all team members
- Virtual or in-person options
- Recommendations for improvements
- Contact: ergonomics@technova.com

4. Equipment Care and Use

4.1 General Care
Take care of company equipment:
- Handle devices carefully
- Keep equipment clean
- Store securely when not in use
- Avoid extreme temperatures
- Don't leave in vehicles unattended

4.2 Acceptable Use
Company equipment may be used for:
- Work-related tasks
- Reasonable personal use (limited)
- Professional development

4.3 Prohibited Use
Company equipment may NOT be used for:
- Illegal activities
- Running personal businesses
- Cryptocurrency mining
- Excessive personal use
- Unauthorized software installation
- Sharing with non-employees

4.4 Software Installation
Software guidelines:
- Only approved software may be installed
- Request new software through IT portal
- IT will review for security and licensing
- Some software pre-approved (see list in IT portal)

5. Security Requirements

5.1 Device Security
All company devices must:
- Have full disk encryption enabled
- Be enrolled in Mobile Device Management (MDM)
- Have automatic updates enabled
- Run approved endpoint protection
- Be password protected

5.2 Password Requirements
For device access:
- Strong password or PIN required
- Biometric authentication encouraged
- Auto-lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Never share login credentials

5.3 Data Protection
Protect company data:
- Store work files in approved cloud storage
- Don't store sensitive data on local drives
- Use VPN when on public networks
- Report any security incidents immediately

5.4 Lost or Stolen Devices
If equipment is lost or stolen:
1. Report immediately to IT: it@technova.com
2. Call 24/7 security line for urgent situations
3. IT will remotely wipe the device
4. File police report if stolen
5. Complete incident report

6. Maintenance and Support

6.1 IT Support
Get help with equipment:
- Slack: #it-help
- Email: it@technova.com
- Phone: For urgent issues only
- Hours: Follow the sun support (24/5)

6.2 Repairs
When equipment needs repair:
- Report issues to IT immediately
- IT will troubleshoot remotely first
- Hardware repairs arranged by IT
- Loaner equipment available if needed
- Don't attempt unauthorized repairs

6.3 Replacement
Equipment replacement:
- Damaged equipment: Report to IT for replacement
- Aging equipment: Refreshed every 3-4 years
- Lost equipment: Report immediately; replacement provided
- Theft: Police report required for replacement

8. Software and Licensing

8.1 Approved Software
Pre-approved software includes:
- Google Workspace (email, docs, etc.)
- Slack
- Zoom
- 1Password
- Standard development tools (engineering)
- Role-specific tools (per department)

8.2 Requesting New Software
To request new software:
1. Submit request in IT portal
2. Provide business justification
3. IT reviews for security and compatibility
4. Procurement handles licensing
5. IT installs and configures

8.3 Licensing Compliance
Regarding software licenses:
- Use only licensed software
- Don't share license keys
- Report any licensing concerns
- Unauthorized software will be removed

9. Equipment Return

9.1 When to Return
Return equipment when:
- Leaving TechNova (any reason)
- Upgrading to new equipment
- Equipment is no longer needed
- Transitioning to a different role

9.2 Return Process
For departing team members:
- IT will provide prepaid shipping label
- Package equipment securely
- Include all accessories and chargers
- Ship within 5 business days of last day
- Confirm tracking with IT

9.3 Data Wiping
Before returning equipment:
- Back up any personal files (if applicable)
- IT will securely wipe all data
- Factory reset performed
- Equipment redeployed or recycled

9.4 Failure to Return
If equipment is not returned:
- Reminder sent after 5 business days
- Escalation after 10 business days
- Cost may be deducted from final pay
- Legal action for unreturned equipment

10. Privacy Considerations

10.1 Company Access
TechNova may access:
- Company-owned devices
- Work files and communications
- Activity logs on company systems
- Device location (for security purposes)

10.2 Personal Privacy
Regarding personal use:
- Limited personal use is permitted
- Personal files may be on company devices
- Recommend keeping personal files separate
- All data on company devices may be accessed

10.3 Monitoring
Company equipment may be monitored for:
- Security threats
- Policy compliance
- Legal requirements
- Performance of IT systems

11. Environmental Responsibility

11.1 E-Waste
Responsible disposal:
- Don't throw electronics in regular trash
- Return old equipment to TechNova
- We recycle through certified partners
- Data securely destroyed before recycling

11.2 Sustainability
Our sustainability efforts:
- Energy-efficient equipment selections
- Extended equipment lifecycle when possible
- Donation of usable equipment
- Recycling of all e-waste

12. Policy Violations

12.1 Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Loss of equipment privileges
- Required training
- Disciplinary action
- Termination for serious violations

12.2 Reporting Violations
Report policy violations:
- To IT for technical issues
- To HR for behavioral concerns
- Anonymous reporting available
