TechNova Solutions - Employee Handbook
Document: Workplace Health and Safety Policy
Last Updated: December 2024
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Health and Safety Commitment

1.1 Our Commitment
TechNova Solutions is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all team members, contractors, visitors, and anyone else who may be affected by our operations. Safety is a core value that we integrate into every aspect of our work.

1.2 Safety Mission
Our health and safety program aims to:
- Prevent workplace injuries and illnesses
- Comply with all applicable safety regulations
- Promote a culture of safety awareness
- Continuously improve safety practices
- Protect the well-being of everyone in our workplace

2. Shared Responsibilities

2.1 Company Responsibilities
TechNova commits to:
- Providing a safe physical work environment
- Maintaining equipment and facilities properly
- Providing necessary safety training
- Investigating incidents and implementing improvements
- Complying with OSHA and local safety regulations
- Providing appropriate safety equipment and resources

2.2 Manager Responsibilities
Managers are responsible for:
- Ensuring team members understand safety procedures
- Identifying and addressing hazards in their areas
- Encouraging reporting of safety concerns
- Supporting safety training completion
- Modeling safe behaviors
- Investigating incidents in their area

2.3 Team Member Responsibilities
All team members must:
- Follow all safety policies and procedures
- Report hazards and incidents promptly
- Use safety equipment as required
- Participate in safety training
- Take responsibility for personal safety
- Look out for colleagues' safety

3. Office Safety

3.1 General Office Safety
In TechNova offices:
- Keep walkways and exits clear
- Clean up spills immediately
- Use step stools for reaching high items
- Report broken furniture or equipment
- Keep desk areas organized
- Close file drawers when not in use

3.2 Ergonomics
Prevent repetitive strain injuries:
- Position monitor at eye level
- Keep keyboard and mouse at elbow height
- Use a chair that supports proper posture
- Take regular breaks from sitting
- Request ergonomic assessment from Facilities

3.3 Electrical Safety
Prevent electrical hazards:
- Don't overload electrical outlets
- Report frayed cords or damaged plugs
- Keep liquids away from electrical equipment
- Unplug equipment before cleaning
- Don't use personal heaters without approval

3.4 Fire Safety
Fire prevention and response:
- Know location of fire exits and extinguishers
- Never block fire exits or emergency equipment
- Report any fire hazards immediately
- Participate in fire drills
- If fire occurs: Evacuate, Call 911, Don't use elevators

4. Remote Work Safety

4.1 Home Office Safety
For remote workers:
- Maintain a safe, organized workspace
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Set up ergonomic workstation
- Keep work area free of tripping hazards
- Have working smoke detectors

4.2 Ergonomic Considerations
Remote work ergonomics:
- Use proper desk and chair (stipend available)
- Position monitor to reduce neck strain
- Take regular movement breaks
- Request ergonomic consultation from HR

4.3 Mental Health
Support for remote workers:
- Set boundaries between work and personal time
- Take regular breaks throughout the day
- Stay connected with colleagues
- Access EAP resources as needed
- Speak up if feeling isolated or overwhelmed

5. Emergency Procedures

5.1 Emergency Contacts
In case of emergency:
- Emergency Services: 911
- Security (offices): Posted at each location
- Facilities Emergency: facilities@technova.com
- After-hours emergency line: 1-800-TECH-911

5.2 Evacuation Procedures
In case of evacuation:
1. Stop what you are doing
2. Leave the building using nearest safe exit
3. Do not use elevators
4. Proceed to designated assembly area
5. Wait for all-clear before returning

5.3 Medical Emergencies
If someone is injured or ill:
1. Call 911 for serious emergencies
2. Do not move the person unless in immediate danger
3. Provide first aid if trained and safe to do so
4. Notify Facilities or Security
5. Stay with the person until help arrives

5.4 Severe Weather
During severe weather:
- Move away from windows
- Go to designated shelter area
- Follow instructions from emergency coordinators
- Remote workers: Follow local emergency guidance

6. Incident Reporting

6.1 What to Report
Report all incidents, including:
- Injuries (no matter how minor)
- Near-misses
- Hazardous conditions
- Property damage
- Security concerns
- Unsafe behaviors

6.2 How to Report
Report incidents by:
- Notifying your manager immediately
- Submitting report in safety portal
- For emergencies: Call 911 first
- Email: safety@technova.com

6.3 Investigation Process
After an incident is reported:
- Safety team investigates promptly
- Root causes are identified
- Corrective actions implemented
- Findings shared to prevent recurrence
- No retaliation for good-faith reporting

7. First Aid

7.1 First Aid Resources
Available resources include:
- First aid kits in all office locations
- AED devices on each floor
- Trained first aid responders identified
- Emergency eyewash stations in labs

7.2 First Aid Training
TechNova provides:
- Optional CPR/First Aid certification
- Annual training for floor wardens
- Emergency response team training
- Online safety awareness courses

8. Substance Abuse

8.1 Drug and Alcohol Policy
TechNova maintains a drug-free workplace:
- Illegal drug use prohibited on company premises
- Being impaired at work is prohibited
- Prescription medications: Use responsibly and disclose if affecting work
- Alcohol: Permitted at sanctioned company events only

8.2 Support for Substance Issues
Help is available:
- EAP provides confidential counseling
- Medical leave may be available for treatment
- Contact HR confidentially for support
- Focus is on help, not punishment for seeking assistance

9. Workplace Violence Prevention

9.1 Zero Tolerance
TechNova has zero tolerance for:
- Physical violence or threats
- Intimidating or bullying behavior
- Weapons on company property
- Harassment of any kind

9.2 Warning Signs
Be alert to warning signs:
- Threats or aggressive statements
- Unusual behavior changes
- Obsessive behavior
- Talk of violence or revenge
- Excessive anger

9.3 Reporting Violence
If you witness or experience violence:
- Remove yourself from danger if possible
- Call 911 for immediate threats
- Report to manager, HR, or security
- Use anonymous reporting if preferred

10. Health and Wellness

10.1 Wellness Programs
TechNova supports your health:
- Wellness stipend ($100/month)
- Gym membership discounts
- Flu shots (free, on-site)
- Health screenings offered
- Mental health resources

10.2 Sick Leave
When you're ill:
- Stay home when sick
- Follow sick leave policy
- No pressure to work while ill
- Return when healthy to prevent spread

10.3 Pandemic Preparedness
In case of health emergencies:
- Follow public health guidance
- Remote work may be required
- Enhanced cleaning procedures
- Travel restrictions may be implemented
- Regular communication from leadership

11. Safety Training

11.1 Required Training

11.2 Specialized Training

12.1 Safety Inspections
Regular inspections include:

12.2 Feedback Welcome

- Report hazards promptly
- Suggest safety improvements
- Participate in safety committees
