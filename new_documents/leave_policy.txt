1. Leave and Time Off Policy

1.1 Overview
TechNova Solutions believes that time away from work is essential for maintaining health, well-being, and sustained productivity. This policy outlines the various types of leave available to team members and the procedures for requesting time off.

1.2 Flexible Paid Time Off (PTO)
TechNova offers a flexible PTO policy that empowers team members to take the time they need while ensuring business continuity.

1.2.1 PTO Guidelines
- There is no set limit on PTO days, but we recommend taking at least 20 days per year
- PTO must be coordinated with your manager and team
- For absences longer than 2 consecutive weeks, approval from your department head is required
- PTO cannot be used as a substitute for sick leave
- Unused PTO does not carry monetary value upon separation

1.2.2 Requesting PTO
To request PTO:
- Submit your request through the HR portal (Workday) at least 2 weeks in advance
- Discuss coverage plans with your manager before submitting
- For peak business periods, additional notice may be required
- Emergency situations will be handled on a case-by-case basis

1.2.3 PTO Best Practices
- Provide notice equal to twice the length of your planned absence (e.g., 4 days notice for 2 days off)
- Document your ongoing projects and handoff responsibilities
- Set up out-of-office replies and update your calendar
- Avoid scheduling PTO during critical project deadlines without prior approval

1.3 Public Holidays
TechNova observes the following paid public holidays:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day (Third Monday in January)
- Presidents' Day (Third Monday in February)
- Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day (First Monday in September)
- Thanksgiving Day (Fourth Thursday in November)
- Day After Thanksgiving (Fourth Friday in November)
- Christmas Eve (December 24)
- Christmas Day (December 25)

For team members outside the United States, equivalent local public holidays apply. You may also choose to observe different holidays that are meaningful to you, with manager approval.

1.4 Sick Leave
TechNova provides paid sick leave for team members who need time to recover from illness or attend medical appointments.

1.4.1 Sick Leave Allowance
- Full-time team members receive up to 15 paid sick days per calendar year
- Sick days do not roll over to the following year
- Sick leave may be used for physical illness, mental health days, or preventive care

1.4.2 Reporting Sick Leave
- Notify your manager as soon as possible when you are unable to work
- Enter sick time in Workday by selecting "Sick Leave"
- For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required
- Extended illness (more than 5 days) should be reported to HR for potential disability coverage

1.5 Family and Medical Leave
TechNova complies with the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) and provides additional support for family and medical situations.

1.5.1 Eligibility
Team members who have worked at TechNova for at least 12 months and 1,250 hours are eligible for FMLA leave.

1.5.2 Covered Situations
- Birth and care of a newborn child
- Placement of a child for adoption or foster care
- Care for an immediate family member with a serious health condition
- Medical leave when unable to work due to a serious health condition
- Qualifying exigencies related to a family member's military service

1.5.3 Leave Duration
- Up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave per 12-month period
- TechNova supplements FMLA with paid leave for the first 6 weeks at 100% salary
- Health insurance benefits continue during FMLA leave

1.6 Parental Leave
TechNova offers generous parental leave for all new parents.

1.6.1 Birth Parents
- 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave
- May be taken consecutively or in blocks within 12 months of birth
- Additional 4 weeks at 60% pay available if needed

1.6.2 Non-Birth Parents
- 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave
- May be taken consecutively or in blocks within 12 months of birth/adoption

1.6.3 Adoption and Foster Care
- 12 weeks of fully paid leave for adoption or foster placement
- Applies to both parents in two-parent households

1.7 Bereavement Leave
TechNova provides paid bereavement leave to support team members during times of loss.

1.7.1 Bereavement Allowance
- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): Up to 10 days paid leave
- Extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt, uncle): Up to 5 days paid leave
- Close friend or colleague: Up to 3 days paid leave
- Pet: Up to 2 days paid leave

1.7.2 Additional Support
- Bereavement leave may be taken flexibly, not necessarily consecutively
- Additional unpaid leave may be granted upon request
- Access to counseling services through our Employee Assistance Program
- No documentation required; we trust our team members

1.8 Jury Duty and Civic Leave
Team members summoned for jury duty or other civic obligations will receive full pay for the duration of service.
- Notify your manager and HR upon receiving a jury summons
- Provide a copy of the summons to HR
- If released early, return to work if practical
- Voting leave: Up to 2 hours paid time for voting on election days

1.9 Military Leave
TechNova supports team members who serve in the military reserves or are called to active duty.
- Unpaid leave for military service as required by law
- Job protection and reinstatement rights upon return
- Continuation of benefits during military leave
- Differential pay to supplement military pay up to regular salary for up to 12 months

1.10 Volunteer Time Off
TechNova encourages community involvement and provides paid volunteer time.
- 3 days (24 hours) of paid volunteer time per year
- Must be with a registered non-profit organization
- Coordinate with your manager in advance
- Share your volunteer experience with the team if comfortable

1.11 Sabbatical Leave
Team members with 5+ years of continuous service are eligible for sabbatical leave.
- 4 weeks of paid sabbatical leave
- May be combined with PTO for longer breaks
- Must be planned at least 3 months in advance
- Available once every 5 years

1.12 Leave Request Process
All leave requests should be submitted through Workday:
1. Log into Workday through Okta
2. Navigate to Time Off section
3. Select the appropriate leave type
4. Enter dates and any required comments
5. Submit for manager approval
6. Receive confirmation via email
