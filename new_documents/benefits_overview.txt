1. Benefits Philosophy

1.1 Our Commitment
TechNova Solutions is committed to providing comprehensive benefits that support the health, financial security, and well-being of our team members and their families. We believe competitive benefits are essential to attracting and retaining top talent.

1.2 Benefits Principles
Our benefits program is guided by:
- Comprehensive Coverage: Meeting diverse needs
- Competitive Value: Benchmarked against industry
- Flexibility: Choice to match individual situations
- Accessibility: Easy to understand and use
- Equity: Fair coverage for all team members

2. Health Insurance

2.1 Medical Coverage
TechNova offers three medical plan options:

Premium PPO Plan:
- Lowest deductible ($250 individual / $500 family)
- Lowest out-of-pocket maximum ($2,000 / $4,000)
- Broadest network of providers
- Higher premium cost

Standard PPO Plan:
- Moderate deductible ($500 / $1,000)
- Moderate out-of-pocket maximum ($4,000 / $8,000)
- Wide network coverage
- Balanced premium cost

High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP):
- Higher deductible ($1,500 / $3,000)
- HSA eligible with company contribution
- Lower premium cost
- Best for healthy individuals/families

2.2 Company Contribution
TechNova covers:
- Employee only: 90% of premium
- Employee + spouse/partner: 80% of premium
- Employee + children: 80% of premium
- Employee + family: 75% of premium

2.3 Dental Coverage
Dental insurance includes:
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, x-rays)
- Basic services: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major services: 50% covered (crowns, bridges)
- Orthodontia: 50% covered (children and adults)
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person

2.4 Vision Coverage
Vision insurance includes:
- Annual eye exam: $10 copay
- Frames: $150 allowance every 24 months
- Lenses: Covered every 12 months
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance
- Laser surgery discount: 15% off LASIK

3. Health Savings and Flexible Spending

3.1 Health Savings Account (HSA)
For HDHP participants:
- Tax-advantaged savings for medical expenses
- TechNova contribution: $500 (individual) / $1,000 (family)
- 2024 contribution limit: $4,150 (individual) / $8,300 (family)
- Funds roll over year to year
- Portable if you leave TechNova

3.2 Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
For non-HDHP participants:
- Pre-tax dollars for eligible expenses
- Healthcare FSA: Up to $3,050 per year
- Use it or lose it (with $610 rollover grace)
- Dependent Care FSA also available

3.3 Dependent Care FSA
For childcare and elder care expenses:
- Pre-tax contributions up to $5,000 per year
- Eligible for licensed daycare, preschool, after-school
- Elder care expenses also eligible
- Must be used within plan year

4. Retirement Benefits

4.1 401(k) Plan
TechNova offers a comprehensive 401(k):
- Immediate eligibility upon hire
- Traditional (pre-tax) and Roth (post-tax) options
- Wide range of investment options
- Self-directed brokerage option available

4.2 Company Match
TechNova matches your contributions:
- 100% match on first 3% of salary
- 50% match on next 2% of salary
- Total potential match: 4% of salary
- Immediate vesting on all contributions

4.3 Contribution Limits (2024)
- Employee contribution limit: $23,000
- Catch-up contribution (50+): Additional $7,500
- Total limit (all sources): $69,000

5. Life and Disability Insurance

5.1 Basic Life Insurance
Provided at no cost:
- 1x annual salary (up to $500,000)
- Accidental death and dismemberment (AD&D) included
- Coverage begins on start date

5.2 Supplemental Life Insurance
Optional additional coverage:
- Up to 5x annual salary
- Spouse coverage available
- Child coverage available
- Evidence of insurability may be required

5.3 Short-Term Disability
Coverage for temporary disabilities:
- 60% of weekly salary
- Benefits begin after 7-day waiting period
- Maximum benefit period: 12 weeks
- Fully paid by TechNova

5.4 Long-Term Disability
Coverage for extended disabilities:
- 60% of monthly salary
- Benefits begin after 90 days
- Coverage continues to age 65
- Fully paid by TechNova
